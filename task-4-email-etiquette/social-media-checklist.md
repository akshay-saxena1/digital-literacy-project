# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

---

## ✅ Do’s

* 👍 **Think Before Posting**
  Take a second to ask yourself: *Would I be okay if anyone saw this later?*

* 🔒 **Protect Your Privacy**
  Keep your personal details safe—only share what you’re comfortable with others knowing.

* ✔️ **Verify Information**
  Double-check facts before sharing so you don’t accidentally spread something false.

* 🤝 **Be Respectful**
  Treat people online the same way you’d treat them in real life—with kindness and respect.

* 🌱 **Build a Positive Image**
  Share things that reflect who you are in a good way—your skills, ideas, and achievements.

---

## ❌ Don’ts

* 🚫 **Don’t Share Sensitive Information**
  Avoid posting things like your phone number, address, or anything too personal.

* ⚠️ **Don’t Post Offensive Content**
  If it could hurt someone or create a bad impression, it’s better not to post it.

* 💬 **Avoid Online Arguments**
  Not every comment needs a reply—sometimes it’s best to just move on.

* ❌ **Don’t Spread Misinformation**
  If you’re not sure it’s true, don’t share it.

* 🙅 **Don’t Overshare**
  You don’t have to post everything—keeping some things private is completely okay.

---

## 💡 Final Tip

👉 Post like your future self is watching—because one day, it might.
