### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

**Tools Used:** Google, National Cyber Crime Portal

**Case Study Topic:** Ransomware

**Case Study:**
Ransomware is a type of cyberattack where a user’s data or system is locked and a ransom is demanded to restore access. It usually spreads through phishing emails or unsafe downloads. For example, a student may receive an email containing an important-looking attachment. Once opened, the malware installs itself and encrypts all files on the device.

A message is then displayed demanding payment (usually in cryptocurrency) in exchange for a decryption key. However, even after payment, access is not guaranteed. Students are often targeted due to lack of awareness and basic security practices.

**Prevention Checklist Includes:**

* Avoid suspicious links and attachments

* Verify sender before downloading files

* Use antivirus and keep systems updated

* Back up important data regularly

* Use strong passwords

* Avoid untrusted downloads

* Verify UPI/payment requests carefully

* Never share OTPs or financial details

* **Report Cybercrime:**
  [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)

**Reflection:**
This task made me realize how common and dangerous cyber threats are. Even a small mistake can lead to serious consequences. It has encouraged me to be more cautious and responsible while using digital platforms.
