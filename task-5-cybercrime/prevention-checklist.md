# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students

A practical checklist to help you stay safe from cyber threats in your daily digital life.

---

## ✅ Do’s

1. 🔐 **Use Strong Passwords**
   Create unique passwords for each account using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

2. 🔄 **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**
   Add an extra layer of security to your accounts wherever possible.

3. 📩 **Be Careful with Emails and Links**
   Always check the sender and avoid clicking on suspicious links.

4. 📱 **Keep Your Devices Updated**
   Regular updates help fix security vulnerabilities.

5. 🦠 **Use Trusted Antivirus Software**
   Install reliable security tools to detect threats early.

6. 📂 **Download Only from Trusted Sources**
   Use official websites or app stores only.

7. 💰 **Protect Your UPI Details**
   Keep your UPI PIN and OTP completely private.

8. 🧾 **Verify Payment Requests Carefully**
   Double-check UPI requests before approving any transaction.

---

## ❌ Don’ts / Avoids

1. 🚫 **Don’t Reuse Passwords**
   Using the same password everywhere makes accounts easier to hack.

2. ⚠️ **Don’t Ignore Security Alerts**
   Warnings from your device or apps should never be ignored.

3. ❌ **Don’t Click Unknown Links**
   Avoid links from unknown emails, messages, or social media.

4. 📥 **Don’t Download Pirated Content**
   Cracked software often contains hidden malware.

5. 🌐 **Don’t Use Public Wi-Fi for Transactions**
   Avoid banking or payments on unsecured networks.

6. 💸 **Don’t Share OTP or UPI PIN**
   No genuine service will ask for this information.

7. 🤝 **Don’t Trust Unknown Payment Requests**
   Scammers often send fake requests to trick users.

8. 📢 **Don’t Overshare Personal Information Online**
   Limit what you post to reduce risk of misuse.

---

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately:

* 🌐 **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* 📞 **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930

---

## 💡 Final Tip

👉 Staying careful online every day is the best way to avoid cyber threats.
